# Geological Indicators
## Elevation Data
- Provided in elevation.png, use `Processing Files/proces_geo.py`
- Resolution: ~500 m
- Range: -85 m to 4421 m
## NDVI Data
- Provided in ndvi.png, use `Processing Files/proces_geo.py`
- Resolution: ~500 m
- Range: -1.0 to 1.0
